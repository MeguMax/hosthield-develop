About us

We help you navigate short term rental laws.

We have successfully litigated against landlords, property owners, and property management companies and have seen the extreme risk carried by business who have inadequate legal protection and flimsy waivers. We also started a real estate investment company which operates several short-term rentals. We began drafting our own liability protection agreements and quickly realized an efficient way to draft, organize, and store waivers did not exist.

It occurred to us that most property owners who operate rentals do not possess the legal expertise to sufficiently protect themselves from liability. The founders’ expertise is the perfect intersection between lawyer and rental host.
